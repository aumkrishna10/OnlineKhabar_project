About Online Khabar

Our Story

Online Khabar was established in 2006 as Nepal's first digital news portal. Since then, we have grown to become the most visited news website in Nepal, delivering timely and accurate news in both Nepali and English languages.

Our Mission

We are committed to providing unbiased, factual, and comprehensive news coverage to our readers. Our team of experienced journalists works around the clock to bring you the latest developments from Nepal and around the world.

Our Team

With over 100 dedicated professionals including reporters, editors, and technical staff, Online Khabar maintains the highest standards of journalism. We have correspondents in all seven provinces of Nepal and key international locations.
